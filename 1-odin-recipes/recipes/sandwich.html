Steps

  1. Start by selecting two slices of fresh bread as the base of your sandwich.
  2. Wash a few leaves of lettuce and pat them dry with a paper towel. Place the lettuce on one slice of bread, covering it completely.
  3. Slice a tomato into medium-thick rounds and lay them over the lettuce, distributing them evenly.
  4. Choose a slice of your favorite cheeseâ€”cheddar is a good option for its melting qualities and sharp taste. Place the cheese on top of the tomato slices.
  5. Take one or two slices of ham and lay them on the cheese. For added flavor, you could consider lightly frying the ham first.
  6. Cover the ingredients with the second slice of bread, pressing down gently to compact the sandwich slightly.
  7. If desired, cut the sandwich diagonally or straight down the middle to make it easier to handle and eat.
